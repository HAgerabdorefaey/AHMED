New car

(Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A.) Lamborghini is generally referred to as an Italian car manufacturer. The company was founded by industry magnate Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, with the aim of producing a car for the Grand Tour and competing with existing offers from other manufacturers such as Ferrari. In the group.(Italian pronunciation: [lamborˈɡiːni]) is an Italian brand and manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs based in Sant'Agata Bolognese. The company is owned by the Volkswagen Group through its subsidiary Audi.

Under the management of Mimrans, the Lamborghini model line of the Contache was expanded to include the Halpa Sports Car and the LM002 High Performance Off-Road Car.

In the late first decade of the twenty-first century, during the global financial crisis and the subsequent economic crisis, Lamborghini sales saw a decline of almost 50%.

In 1985, British importer Lamborghini Koontach developed the QVX, jointly with Spice Engineering, for the Group C championship season.
